📚 Learn Chess

♟️ Openings

Openings are the first moves of a chess game. Popular openings include the Italian Game, Sicilian Defense, and Queen's Gambit.

⚔️ Middlegame

The middlegame focuses on strategy, tactics, and positioning. Players aim to control the center and create attacking opportunities.

🏁 Endgame

The endgame occurs when few pieces remain. Knowing checkmate patterns and pawn promotion is key to winning.
